GIS

Triad has developed an intimate knowledge of the GIS Recruitment market: we understand our clients' resourcing requirements at all levels. In the last 3 years we have placed over 300 candidates with 50 clients.

Whilst Triad's resourcing capability covers the broad spectrum of IT disciplines, we have developed a specialist practice in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) that has now been established for over 14 years.

Triad Resourcing has extensive experience of supplying to both the public and private sectors. We operate a flexible resourcing model and can supply contractors, permanent and contract to permanent staff. We also have particular expertise in supplying large project teams for digitisation and data capture projects

We can supply across all GIS platforms and technologies.

Case Study: Rapid Deployment of Teams

Triad has developed a close relationship with a provider of teams of GIS technicians to a public sector agency. Close liaison with our client enables us to source and qualify large numbers of candidates before the client presses the button, enabling us to then supply a large project team within a short timescale.

Triad's GIS expertise includes product as well as people, and we have developed our own geospatial platform called zubed geospatial.
