Welcome to Triad Resourcing

The specialist supplier of IT contract and permanent recruitment services

Triad Resourcing is part of Triad Group Plc. We are a UK based IT recruitment agency with offices in Surrey and Milton Keynes.

The Resourcing and Consulting & Solutions divisions of Triad Group combine to deliver a comprehensive client focused IT service.

Established for over 20 years we have a proven track record across all sectors. We use our experience and expertise to find the right people for your business.

Triad Resourcing is a leading IT recruitment agency with the following IT recruitment services:

  • Contract resourcing
  • Permanent recruitment
  • Interim managers
