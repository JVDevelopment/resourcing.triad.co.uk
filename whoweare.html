Our Heritage

In 2013 Triad Group Plc, which is listed on the main London Stock Exchange, celebrated its 25th anniversary. 2013 also marked the 20th anniversary of our resourcing business.

Triad Resourcing has earned a reputation for the expertise of its experienced consultants, the reliability of its systems and processes, and its rigorous standards of compliance and we are very proud of our heritage.

However we never stand still and are always striving to innovate and to meet the changing needs of our clients.

Fusion

Our experienced recruitment consultants have developed a detailed knowledge across a wide spectrum of technologies. This is underpinned by the expertise of our own Consulting & Solutions business' consultants, who are involved every day with cutting edge technologies, and who provide a conduit for information on the latest technological developments.

Access to our own pool of technology consultants means that we can offer a range of resourcing solutions including a blend of permanent and non-permanent staff. This model provides a number of benefits to clients, including being able to combine continuity whilst also being able to respond according to fluctuating demand.
